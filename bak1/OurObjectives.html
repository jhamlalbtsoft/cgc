Our Objectives

Chhattisgarh Chamber of Commerce & Industries has been playing a pivotal role in promoting trade. Chhattisgarh Chambers of Commerce & Industries seeks to achieve the following objectives:

  • To Unite the Trade and Industry of the area and help them in solving their Problems.
  • To help Trade & Industry for removal of their difficulties.
  • Liaising of Trade and Industry with State and Central Govt. Department.
  • To safe guard the interest of Trade, Commerce and Industries.
  • To help and promote Cultural, Literary, Social, Educational, Sports activities.
